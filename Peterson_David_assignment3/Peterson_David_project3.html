Project 3

One of the great achievments in the Adirondacks is hiking the 46 peaks with an summit elevation over 4,000 feet.

The one who hikes them is called a "Forty-Sixer."

This project was difficult in that for me there was a tension between "what is the most efficient way" vs. "what processes meet the rubic."

I felt like I kept breaking apart the code to make functions, then complicating them to meet the specs.

This is not a complaint, just that I felt like I was redoing thing that already worked.

I will say that I really learned a lot.

March 21, 2013, David Peterson
